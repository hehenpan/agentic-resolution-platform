# General Ecommerce Cancelling or Modifying an Order

## Cancelling or modifying an order

If you wish to cancel or modify your order, we can usually assist provided that your order has not already been dispatched from our warehouse or one of our suppliers warehouses. You can view the progress of your order from the Online Orders page of your online General Ecommerce account or by contacting us directly.

## Modifying your order

If an order needs to be changed, the usual process is to cancel the current order and place a new order with the correct items. If you require assistance with changing your order, please call our customer support team on 09-0000-001.

At General Ecommerce we value customers and aim to provide a helpful, reliable shopping experience. If you are cancelling your order as you are dissatisfied or unhappy with the service you have received, please let us know so we can have the opportunity to resolve your problem and make your next shopping experience smoother.

## Cancelling your order

You can request cancellation of your order via the cancel button (shown below) on the Order page, in your online General Ecommerce account. You can find all your online orders on the Online Orders page. If you do not have an online General Ecommerce account, please contact us and request to have your order cancelled. Once you have requested for your order to be cancelled, a member of our team will review your order and update the status of your order. If you have already paid for your order, a member of our team will contact you to arrange for a refund, once your order has been cancelled.
