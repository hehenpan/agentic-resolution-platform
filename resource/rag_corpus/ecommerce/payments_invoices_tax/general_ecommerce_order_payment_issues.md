# General Ecommerce Order Payment Issues

## My order has not shipped

Several reasons may explain why your order may not have been shipped. We aim to dispatch customer orders as quickly as practical and continue working to move each order through fulfilment. The sections below explain common reasons an order may not yet have shipped to the delivery address, and customers can contact support for more detail.

## Your item is not in stock (Awaiting Goods)

We are only able to dispatch items to the delivery address that are in stock in our warehouse. All items that are for sale on the General Ecommerce website show stock levels on each individual product page. Please review the stock information before completing your purchase.

If there is a specific item you have ordered and is on backorder you will get an automated email with a estimated availability date of when we will have it in stock, please note that these dates are estimates and may vary by product.

## There is a problem with your credit card

Please ensure that you double check your credit card details including, expiry dates and sufficient amount of funds before placing an order, as incorrectly entered details are a usual problem.

Please allow 1-2 hours during normal business hours for us to re-try your credit card if there has been a problem.

## Payment has not been received

If you have paid by direct bank transfer it is possible that we haven't yet received your payment. Please make sure you double check you have included your order number as a reference with your payment.

Please be aware that it usually takes 1-2 working days to appear in our bank account.

If a payment was made using an incorrect reference please contact support@generalecommerce.com so the payment can be matched to the order.
