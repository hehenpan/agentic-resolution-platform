# General Ecommerce Delivery Rates, Times and Options

## Delivery rates, times & options

## Delivery Estimates

Each product page shows an estimated dispatch timeframe near the listed price. From when your items ship, products typically arrive within 1-2 working days for North Island deliveries and 2-3 working days for South Island deliveries. Rural deliveries may take an extra working day. Bulk & hazard deliveries may take an extra 2-7 working days and tracking links for these deliveries can take a few days to generate.

## Delivery Partners

General Ecommerce uses a range of courier and freight partners in New Zealand and selected overseas destinations. These may include national postal carriers, regional couriers, freight providers, and urgent delivery services. The courier partner used for an order depends on the delivery location, requested timeframe, parcel size, item type, and service availability. General Ecommerce will email a tracking link once a courier partner has collected the item or items from us. The tracking link can also be found on the order details page of the customer's online General Ecommerce account.

## Same Day Delivery

Need an order urgently? General Ecommerce offers same day delivery (not available everywhere) when the items are available in stock. Select one of our same day delivery options Uber Delivery, Evening Delivery or Urgent 3-hour Delivery in the checkout. Terms and conditions apply. Learn more about Same Day Delivery.

## Shipping Security & Insurance

All orders shipped by General Ecommerce are sent via a courier with a signature required for each delivery. In some cases, and only where you have given the courier company permission to leave orders at a designated location, your order may be delivered without requiring a signature. All orders sent by General Ecommerce are fully insured if your item(s) are damaged or are lost in transit.

## Overseas Shipments

General Ecommerce supports selected international shipments. For Australian customers, you can order directly from our Australian site www.generalecommerce.com/au. Customers in other countries you can order from www.generalecommerce.com

## Split Shipping Orders With Out-Of-Stock Items

Orders containing out-of-stock items will usually be split shipped. This means available in-stock items may be shipped first, instead of holding the entire order until every item is available, before shipping the order. Some business customers will have the option in the checkout to not split ship their order.

## Shipping Costs

Shipping costs depend on the delivery location and the items included in the shipment. In some cases shipping may even be FREE, if mentioned on the product page or as part of a promotional offer.

To estimate what the standard shipping costs will be for your order, add the relevant items to your cart, view the Shopping Cart page, and select the applicable delivery area to estimate the shipping cost.

Non-standard shipping costs such as rural, bulk and faster delivery options will be more expensive. All shipping costs are displayed on the Shipping page of the Checkout.
